Data Collection
Stay Home Notice Reporting Application

The data collected by the application includes:
  1. Mobile phone number.

  2. Postal code of place of residence.

  3. Distance from place of residence.

  4. Temperature readings that are reported by the user.

  5. Symptoms that are reported by the user.

Please contact info@imda.gov.sg for any further enquires or feedback.
